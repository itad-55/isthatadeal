Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

isthatadeal.ca uses a minimal set of cookies. This page explains what cookies are set, why, and how you can control them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They are used to remember preferences and understand how visitors interact with a site.

Cookies We Use

We use only analytics cookies via Google Analytics. We do not use advertising, tracking, or personalisation cookies of any kind.

Cookie Provider Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics Distinguishes unique visitors 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics Maintains session state 2 years

Google Analytics collects anonymous information about how visitors use the site — such as which pages are visited, how long visitors stay, and general location (city/region level). This data helps us understand what's useful and improve the site. No personally identifiable information is included.

What We Don't Use

  • No advertising or retargeting cookies
  • No social media tracking cookies
  • No third-party marketing or data broker cookies
  • No session cookies tracking individual user behaviour across sites

How to Control Cookies

You can control or disable cookies at any time:

  • Browser settings — Most browsers allow you to view, block, or delete cookies. See your browser's help documentation for instructions.
  • Google Analytics opt-out — Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to prevent your data from being used by Google Analytics across all sites.

Note: Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to use this site or receive our newsletter.

Changes to This Policy

If we add new cookies or change how we use them, we will update this page with a new date. We aim to keep cookie use to a minimum.
